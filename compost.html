How We Recycle Organic Waste into Compost

At ScrapKart, we use eco-friendly methods to convert organic waste into nutrient-rich compost.

1. Waste Collection

We collect organic waste from households, schools, and businesses.

2. Composting Process

The waste is placed in compost pits where it decomposes naturally over time.

3. High-Quality Compost

After a few weeks, the organic matter turns into nutrient-rich compost, ready for use.
