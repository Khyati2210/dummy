About Us

ScrapKart is an innovative online platform dedicated to waste management and recycling. We collect scrap from households, schools, and other institutions, ensuring a cleaner and greener environment. Our team picks up the scrap and sells it to recycling businesses, generating profits while promoting sustainability.

Our mission is to make scrap disposal easy and efficient while contributing to environmental well-being. Join us in making the world a cleaner place, one scrap at a time!
